## Overview

Reflect Notes Advanced is an AI-powered networked note-taking application that combines the bidirectional linking approach of modern knowledge management with built-in AI writing assistance and end-to-end encrypted synchronization in a beautifully minimal interface.

Backlinks surface every note that references the current one automatically, building a navigable network of related ideas without manual organization overhead that other linked note systems require before the connections become useful.

AI integration enables summarization, expansion, question answering, and writing assistance directly within the note interface, making AI tools a natural part of the note-taking process rather than a separate application to switch between.

End-to-end encryption protects note content in transit and at rest, ensuring that your ideas remain private and accessible only to you even when synchronized across devices through Reflect's cloud infrastructure.

For users who want the connectivity of a linked note system with the practical AI assistance that modern writing tools offer, in an application that prioritizes privacy and a genuinely beautiful writing experience, Reflect Notes Advanced delivers all of it.

## Performance

Reflect Notes balances performance with system resource usage intelligently. Operations are handled in the background, keeping your Mac responsive for everything else. Efficient memory management reduces overhead during extended work sessions.

Apple Silicon acceleration is fully utilized on M-series Macs, delivering noticeably faster performance compared to software-only solutions. On Intel machines, hardware acceleration is used wherever available.
